Owen's favourite TV shows

My top 3 TV shows of all different genres in no particular order

Breaking Bad

This is my favourite 'drama' show; the acting in the show is excellent, the tension built up by the different camera angles is perfect and it demonstrates the transition from a 'good guy' to a villian based on terrible circumstances which make him break 'bad' essentially.

The Office

A classic comedic sitcom, which never fails to make me laugh. The Office is my goto show when I can't find something good to watch while I'm eating.
10/10.

Mr Robot

A show that reveals the evil aspect of top corporations that you see everyday. Mr Robot is about a hacking group called 'FSociety' who attempt to make the corporations that essentially run the world pay for their terrible acts. I will not reveal too much about the show, all I will say is, watch it.
9/10.
